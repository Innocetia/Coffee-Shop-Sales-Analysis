# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analysis

üìå Project Overview

This project analyzes coffee shop sales data to identify revenue trends, product performance and customer purchasing behavior across store locations, time of day, and product categories. An interactive dashboard was built to support data-driven business insights and recommendations.

üéØ Objectives

- Analyze sales performance by store location, time of day, and product category

- Identify high-performing and low-performing products

- Provide actionable business recommendations based on insights

- Miro (Project Planning)

- Canva (Presentation)

üìä Key Insights

- Coffee records the highest unit sales, with peak demand in the morning

- Bakery products show strong performance, often purchased alongside coffee and tea

- Certain categories (Packaged Chocolate, Branded products, Loose Tea) consistently contribute low sales

- Revenue peaked in March and declined sharply in April, indicating possible seasonal or operational factors

üí° Recommendations

- Optimize staffing and inventory during peak morning hours

- Focus product strategy on high-performing categories while reassessing low-performing items

- Apply location-specific strategies to improve overall sales performance

- Use monthly trends to support proactive planning and promotional decisions
